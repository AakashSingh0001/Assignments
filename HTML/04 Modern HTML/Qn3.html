Definition: Accessibility in web Development

Accessibilityin web development refers to the practice of designing and developing website, application, and digital content in a way that everyone can access and use them, including people with disabilitiessuch as visule, auditory, physical, speech, congnitive, and neurilogical conditions.

Why Accessibility Is Essential

Creating accessible website is not just about meeting legal requirements -- it's about ensuring equal access to information and services for all users. It promotes inclusivity and removes barriers that could prevent someone from fully using a website.

Benefits for others User Groups

  • Elderly users:Often have vision, hearing, or mobility challenges.
  • Mobile users:smaller screens and touch interface benefit from clear, scalable, and simple interface.
  • Temporary impairments:A user with a broken arm or in a noisy environment still needs access.
  • Slow internet connections:Lightweight, accessible design often liad faster
