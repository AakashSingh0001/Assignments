PW Skills

PW Skills is an online learning platform developed by Physics Wallah, an Indian edtech company founded by Alakh Pandey in 2016. Initially starting as a YouTube channel focused on physics education, Physics Wallah expanded into a comprehensive educational platform, offering a variety of courses aimed at bridging the gap between academic learning and industry demands .
